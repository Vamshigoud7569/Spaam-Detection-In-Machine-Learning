Abstract

Welcome to Email Spam Detection Project

This project aims to revolutionize email spam detection through innovative integration of Genetic Algorithm (GA) and Harris Hawk Optimization (HHO) algorithm.

Our approach leverages GA's evolutionary principles to optimize feature selection and model parameters, thereby enhancing the efficiency of spam classification. Furthermore, inspired by the hunting behavior of Harris's Hawks, HHO contributes to further optimization of the algorithm.

By combining these evolutionary techniques, we aim to significantly improve accuracy and reduce false positives in email spam detection, providing a robust solution to combat evolving spam tactics.

The synergy between GA and HHO enhances the overall robustness and adaptability of the spam detection system, offering a novel approach to address the evolving nature of email spam. Our project's methodology, experimental results, and comparative analysis contribute to advancing the field of email security and cyber threat detection.

Explore the effectiveness of bio-inspired algorithms for spam mail detection and the importance of rigorous testing in ensuring system performance and usability.
